# Logo Setup Guide

## Adding the KEEPSTRONG Logo

1. **Save the logo image** to `/public/logo.png` or `/public/logo.svg`
   - Recommended: PNG with transparent background
   - Recommended size: 512x512px or higher (for retina displays)
   - Format: PNG (with transparency) or SVG

2. **File location**: `/public/logo.png` or `/public/logo.svg`

3. **The logo will automatically appear in**:
   - Sidebar (desktop & mobile)
   - Landing page header and footer
   - All navigation areas

## Logo Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (with transparency) or SVG
- **Size**: 512x512px minimum (for high-DPI displays)
- **Background**: Transparent preferred
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1)

## Alternative Formats

If you have the logo in a different format:
- **SVG**: Best for scalability, place at `/public/logo.svg`
- **PNG**: Good for complex graphics, place at `/public/logo.png`
- **WebP**: Modern format, place at `/public/logo.webp`

The code will automatically detect and use the logo file.
